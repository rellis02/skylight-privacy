SkyLight Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026
Developer: Robert Ellis
Application: SkyLight

1. Overview

SkyLight is an astronomy application that displays stars, planets, and satellites based on the device’s orientation and geographic location.

SkyLight does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

2. Device Permissions and Usage

Location

SkyLight accesses the device’s approximate or precise location solely to calculate accurate celestial positions relative to the user’s viewing position.

  • Location data is processed locally on the device
  • Location data is not stored
  • Location data is not transmitted to any external server
  • Location data is not shared with third parties

Device Sensors

SkyLight uses device sensors (including rotation vector, gyroscope, and magnetometer) to align the sky display with the direction the device is pointed.

  • Sensor data is processed locally
  • Sensor data is not stored
  • Sensor data is not transmitted

Internet Access

SkyLight may access the internet to download publicly available satellite orbital data (such as Two-Line Element (TLE) files).

  • No personal information is transmitted
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising services are integrated
  • No background data collection occurs

3. Data Collection Statement

SkyLight does not:

  • Create user accounts
  • Collect personal information
  • Store personal information
  • Use analytics services
  • Use advertising SDKs
  • Use crash reporting tools
  • Share information with third parties

All calculations and processing occur entirely on the user’s device.

4. Third-Party Services

SkyLight is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect information in accordance with its own Privacy Policy. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for details regarding Play Store data practices.

5. Children’s Privacy

SkyLight does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Data Security

Because SkyLight does not collect or retain personal data, no personal data is stored by the developer.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if application functionality changes. Updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date above.
